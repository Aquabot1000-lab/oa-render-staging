Section 5 — Protest Argument

Grounds for Protest: Unequal Appraisal (TX Tax Code §42.26) and Market Value Exceeds Actual Value


The property at 2022 Avondown Rd, Forney, TX is currently assessed at $418,000 for the 2026 tax year — a 34% increase since 2022 ($311,896). This assessment is both above market value and unequal compared with similar properties in the same neighborhood.


Equal & Uniform Analysis: An evaluation of 15 comparable properties on Avondown Rd and surrounding streets — all within the same subdivision, same property type, similar size (2,400–3,300 sq ft) — shows a median adjusted value of $292,600. Adjustments were made for differences in square footage, age, land value, pool presence, and features. The subject property at $105.20/sq ft exceeds the median comparable rate of $100.35/sq ft.


Market Value Analysis: Five recent comparable sales (Aug 2025 – Mar 2026) within 0.6 miles show prices ranging from $299,000 to $372,500, with a median of $325,000. The Rentcast automated valuation model estimates the subject at $363,000. Both figures are significantly below the $418,000 assessment.


Recommended Value: $292,600 — representing a $125,400 reduction and estimated annual tax savings of $2,822.
