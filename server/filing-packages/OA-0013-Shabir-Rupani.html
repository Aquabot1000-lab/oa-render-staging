Section 5 — Protest Argument

Grounds for Protest: Market Value Exceeds Actual Value


The property at 708 Santa Lucia Dr, Anna, TX is assessed at $279,990 for 2026. Comparison with 5 similar properties on Santa Lucia Dr and adjacent streets in the same Anna, TX subdivision shows a median assessed value of $256,491.


Using the Collin CAD's own property records, comparable homes in the immediate neighborhood are valued 8-11% lower than the subject. The weighted average of adjusted values is $259,736, supporting a reduction to $256,491.


Note: This is a marginal case. The $23,499 reduction yields $517/yr in tax savings. Recent market sales suggest the property may actually be undervalued relative to the sales market, which could be raised by the ARB. Recommend filing but setting expectations with the client accordingly.
