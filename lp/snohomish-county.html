Why Snohomish County Homes Are Over-Assessed

Seattle spillover has distorted local assessments

🌲

North Sound Growth

As Seattle prices climbed, Everett, Marysville, and surrounding cities attracted buyers seeking more space. Rapid appreciation means assessors often overshoot true market value.

📊

100% Market Value

Washington assesses at full market value. On a $600K home at ~1% tax rate, a 10% over-assessment costs ~$600 per year in excess taxes - money you deserve to keep.

🏛️

Snohomish County BOE

The Snohomish County Board of Equalization reviews appeals and has the authority to reduce your assessed value. We present your case with comparable sales and market evidence.

🏗️

New Construction Confusion

Heavy development in the area means the assessor may use new-build comps that don't reflect your existing home's true value. We find the right comparables for your property.
