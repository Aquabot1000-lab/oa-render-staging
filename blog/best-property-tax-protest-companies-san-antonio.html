How to Choose the Right Company

When evaluating property tax protest companies, focus on these factors:

  1. Fee percentage: This is the biggest variable. On $1,000 in savings, the difference between 20% and 33% is $130/year — $650 over 5 years.
  2. Hearing representation: Make sure the company attends both informal and formal hearings. Some cheaper services only file the protest but don't represent you at hearings.
  3. Evidence quality: data-driven analysis (used by OverAssessed and Ownwell) typically finds more and better comparable properties than manual research alone.
  4. No-win, no-fee guarantee: All reputable companies offer this. If someone asks for money upfront, walk away.
  5. Local expertise: A company that understands Bexar County's specific processes and appraisers has an advantage.
