Property Tax Protest — Fee Agreement

OverAssessed, LLC

1. Services

OverAssessed, LLC ("Company") will analyze your property's assessed value, prepare and file a property tax protest, and represent you at hearings if applicable.

2. Fee Structure

Initiation Fee: $79.00 — Due before filing. Non-refundable. Credited in full toward your success fee.

Success Fee: 25% of first year's tax savings achieved through protest.

Example: $2,000 savings → $500 fee − $79 credit = $421 due

If the protest does not result in savings, no success fee is owed. The $79 initiation fee is non-refundable.

3. Authorization

By signing, you authorize the Company to act as your representative for property tax protest purposes, access property records, file protests, and communicate with appraisal districts on your behalf.

4. No Guarantee

The Company makes no guarantee of a specific outcome. Results depend on market conditions, appraisal district policies, and comparable data.

5. Payment Terms

The $79 initiation fee is due immediately after signing. The success fee is due within 30 days of successful protest notification.

6. Term

This agreement covers the current tax year. Either party may terminate in writing. Filed protests and achieved savings remain subject to fees.
