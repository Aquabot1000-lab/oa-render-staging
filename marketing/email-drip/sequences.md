## Sequence Settings & Notes

### Merge Tags Required
- `{{first_name}}` — subscriber first name
- `{{property_address}}` — property street address
- `{{assessed_value}}` — current assessed value (formatted: $XXX,XXX)
- `{{assessed_value_short}}` — shorter version for inline text
- `{{estimated_value}}` — OverAssessed estimated fair value
- `{{estimated_savings}}` — estimated annual tax savings
- `{{current_taxes}}` — current annual property taxes
- `{{estimated_taxes}}` — estimated taxes after reduction
- `{{year}}` — current tax year

### Timing Adjustments
- If signup is **after May 1**, compress to: Welcome (immediate), Success Story (Day 1), Deadline (Day 2), Last Chance (day before May 15)
- If signup is **after May 13**, send Welcome + Last Chance immediately
- Off-season signups: swap deadline urgency for "get ahead of next year" messaging
