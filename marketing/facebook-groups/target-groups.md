## Engagement Strategy

### Phase 1: Join & Lurk (Week 1)
- Join 5–7 groups to start (don't mass-join, looks suspicious)
- Read posts, understand the vibe and rules
- Note which groups allow promotional posts and which don't
- Identify group admins and active members

### Phase 2: Engage & Build Credibility (Weeks 2–3)
- Comment helpfully on 3–5 posts per day across groups
- Answer questions about home-related topics
- React to and share other people's content
- Introduce yourself if there's a "welcome" thread

### Phase 3: Share Value Content (Weeks 3+)
- Post educational content about property taxes (no links initially)
- Share the "how to protest" guide as a helpful PSA
- Respond to people complaining about taxes with helpful tips
- Mention OverAssessed naturally when someone asks for recommendations

## Rules to Follow
1. **Never spam.** One post per group per week MAX.
2. **Never post the same content** in multiple groups word-for-word (Facebook flags this).
3. **Always check group rules** before posting anything promotional.
4. **Engage 5x more than you promote.** For every promotional post, make 5 helpful comments.
5. **Use personal account**, not business page (higher reach, more trust).
6. **If an admin messages you**, be respectful and comply immediately.
