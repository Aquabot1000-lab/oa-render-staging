# Google Local Service Ads (LSA) — Setup Guide for OverAssessed

## Overview

Google LSA puts you at the **very top** of search results with a "Google Guaranteed" or "Google Screened" badge. You pay per lead (phone call or message), not per click. Ideal for local service businesses like property tax consulting.

## Step 4: Enroll in Local Service Ads

### 4a. Go to ads.google.com/local-services-ads
1. Click "Get Started"
2. Sign in with the Google account tied to your Business Profile
3. Select your business category
4. Enter service area (San Antonio metro — can specify zip codes or radius)

### 4b. Set Your Service Types
Select all that apply:
- [ ] Property tax protest / appeal
- [ ] Property tax consulting
- [ ] Tax reduction services
- [ ] Property valuation review

### 4c. Set Your Budget

| Setting | Recommended |
|---------|-------------|
| **Weekly budget** | $140–$210/week ($20–$30/day) |
| **Monthly budget** | ~$600–$900/month |
| **Max bid per lead** | Let Google auto-set initially, then adjust |

**Budget notes:**
- Start at **$20/day** for the first 2 weeks to gauge lead quality
- Scale to **$30/day** once you confirm CPL is acceptable
- During peak season (March–May), consider increasing to $40–$50/day

### 4d. Set Business Hours for Lead Receiving
- **Hours:** Mon–Fri, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM CST
- Enable voicemail/after-hours messaging if available
- Respond to all leads within **15 minutes** during business hours (Google tracks responsiveness)

### 4e. Submit for Review
- Upload all documents from Step 2
- Complete background check authorization
- Google reviews and approves within **1–3 weeks**

## Step 6: Lead Management

### Response Protocol
1. **Answer calls live** during business hours (Google penalizes missed calls)
2. **Return missed calls** within 15 minutes
3. **Respond to messages** within 5 minutes if possible
4. **Mark leads** in the LSA dashboard:
   - ✅ Booked — converted to customer
   - ❌ Not useful — wrong service, spam, etc. (request credit)
   - 📞 Follow-up needed

### Dispute Invalid Leads
- You can dispute leads that are spam, wrong service, or out of area
- Google credits your account for valid disputes
- Dispute within **30 days** of receiving the lead
