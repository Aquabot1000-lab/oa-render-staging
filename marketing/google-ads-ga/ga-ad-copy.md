## Pin Recommendations

For all RSAs, pin these positions:
- **Headline 1:** Brand/service identifier (e.g., "Georgia Tax Appeal Experts" or county variant)
- **Headline 2:** Key benefit (e.g., "No Upfront Cost to Appeal" or "Freeze Your Value 3 Years")
- **Description 1:** Primary value prop with zero-cost emphasis

Let Google optimize the remaining positions.

---

## A/B Testing Plan

**Week 1–2:** Run all 3 RSAs across their respective ad groups
**Week 3–4:** Review performance by:
- CTR (goal: >4%)
- Conversion rate (goal: >5%)
- Asset performance ratings (Best / Good / Low)

**Ongoing:** Replace "Low" performing headlines/descriptions with new variants. Test new angles:
- Urgency (deadline-based)
- Social proof ("Join thousands of GA homeowners")
- Specificity ("Average $1,200 savings")
