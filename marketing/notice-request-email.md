## GENERIC TEMPLATE (GA)

**Subject:** Action Needed: Forward Your Property Assessment Notice

Hi {{first_name}},

Your county will be mailing your **Annual Notice of Assessment** in the coming months (typically June-July). This is the document that shows your property's new assessed value — and we need it to file your appeal.

**When you receive it, please forward it to us immediately** by photo, email, or just send us the parcel ID.

Georgia gives us **45 days from the notice date** to file your appeal. And here's the great part: under Georgia Code §48-5-299(c), if we win your appeal, **your value is frozen for 3 years**. That means savings that compound.

We'll handle the entire process from there.
