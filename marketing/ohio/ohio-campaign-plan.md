# Ohio Ad Campaign Plan - Franklin County

## Campaign Overview

This plan outlines the setup for a targeted Facebook/Meta ad campaign focused on property owners in Franklin County, Ohio. The objective is to generate leads for property tax assessment appeals.

## Campaign Details

*   **Campaign Name:** Ohio Property Tax Appeal - Franklin County (Spring 2026 Sprint)
*   **Objective:** Lead Generation
*   **Target Audience:** Franklin County, OH homeowners aged 30-65 with interests in home ownership, property taxes, and real estate.
*   **Budget:** $25 per day (Total sprint budget: $125)
*   **Schedule:** 5-day sprint. Recommend starting on Monday, March 30, 2026, to align with the March 31st deadline.
    *   **Start Date:** 2026-03-30
    *   **End Date:** 2026-04-03

## Ad Set Setup

*   **Ad Set Name:** Franklin County Homeowners (30-65)
*   **Location:** Franklin County, Ohio, USA
    *   Targeting: "People living in this location"
*   **Age:** 30-65
*   **Detailed Targeting (Interests/Demographics):**
    *   Homeownership (Demographic)
    *   Real Estate
    *   Property Taxes
    *   Mortgage
    *   (Optional: Add related interests like "Housing Market", "Real Estate Investment", "Local Government")
*   **Placements:** Automatic Placements (Recommended for Meta's optimization across Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and Audience Network).

## Campaign Monitoring & Optimization

*   Monitor ad performance daily within the Meta Ads Manager.
*   After the initial 5-day sprint, analyze results to determine if the campaign should continue, scale, or be paused. Key metrics to watch: Cost Per Lead (CPL), Click-Through Rate (CTR), and Conversion Rate.
*   Ensure the landing page (`overassessed.ai/ohio`) is optimized for conversions and has Meta Pixel tracking set up to accurately measure leads.
