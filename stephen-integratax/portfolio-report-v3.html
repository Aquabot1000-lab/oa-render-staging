Methodology and legal information

Methodology

This portfolio assessment analyzes each property using multiple approaches including Equal & Uniform (E&U) comparable analysis under Texas Tax Code §42.26, market value comparison, income capitalization, and condition/depreciation review. Data is sourced from Bexar County Appraisal District (BCAD) 2025 certified values via bexar.trueautomation.com.

Equal & Uniform Analysis

Under Texas Tax Code §42.26, a property owner may protest that their property's appraised value exceeds the median appraised value of comparable properties appropriately adjusted. Our E&U analysis:

Size Adjustment = (Comp_PSF × (Subject_Area − Comp_Area)) / 2
Age Adjustment = Adjusted_Value × 0.5 × (Subject_EffYear − Comp_EffYear) / 100
Land Adjustment = Subject_Land_Value − Comp_Land_Value
Indicated Value = Median of all adjusted comparable values

Tax Rate Information

Tax rates used: City of San Antonio properties at 2.290174%. Potranco Rd (outside city limits) at 1.836516%. Actual savings will depend on 2026 tax rates set by local taxing authorities.
